--- Critical insight ---
In DIAGNOSTIC setting (CPP vs PT, after referral):
  Basal LH: AUC = 0.915-0.927 (Chen 2022, Zhao 2025)
  IGF-1:    AUC = 0.740-0.880 (Chen 2022, Kim 2025)
  -> LH > IGF-1 in diagnostic setting (expected: LH IS the gold standard)

In SCREENING setting (mixed clinic, before diagnosis, THIS STUDY):
  LH alone:         AUC = 0.529 (near chance)
  Bone age advance:  AUC = 0.827
  Height-Z (NHANES): AUC = 0.866
  -> Growth axis >> gonadotropin axis in screening setting

RECONCILIATION: LH is excellent for CONFIRMING CPP after referral
(diagnostic), but useless for IDENTIFYING future CPP before symptoms
(screening). Growth-axis markers are the opposite: modest for
confirmation but excellent for early identification. This is because
LH rises LATE (only after GnRH pulse generator activates), while
bone age advances EARLY (skeletal response to low-level sex steroids).

SUMMARY: LITERATURE VALIDATION
======================================================================
6 published studies (2017-2025, N = 116 to 5,901) compared.

Key finding: The apparent contradiction between our results
(growth-axis > gonadotropins) and existing literature
(gonadotropins > growth-axis) is resolved by CONTEXT:

  DIAGNOSTIC setting (confirming CPP after clinical referral):
    Basal LH AUC = 0.915-0.927 (dominant)
    IGF-1 AUC = 0.740-0.880 (supportive)
    -> LH is the right tool for CONFIRMING a suspected diagnosis

  SCREENING setting (identifying future CPP in mixed clinic):
    LH AUC = 0.529 (useless)
    Bone age AUC = 0.827, Height-Z AUC = 0.866 (dominant)
    -> Growth-axis is the right tool for EARLY IDENTIFICATION

This context-dependence is the NOVEL CONTRIBUTION:
  'The optimal PP biomarker depends on whether you are
   confirming (use LH) or predicting (use bone age + height).'

Corroborating evidence:
  1. Oliveira 2017: BA advancement predicts GnRH stim result (AUC=0.605)
  2. Chen 2022: IGF-1 alone AUC=0.880 for CPP diagnosis
  3. Meta-analysis 2024: models with imaging > hormones alone
  4. Kim 2025: IGF-1 SDS has screening value (AUC=0.740)
  5. Pan 2019: ML model (AUC=0.886) with bone age as key feature
